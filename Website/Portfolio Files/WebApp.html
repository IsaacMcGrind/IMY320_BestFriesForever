▪A tenant is a single organization where their data and user's data is seperated from other tenants on the Bean Bag platform.

▪White labeling is also a feature of Bean bag in which a tenant admin can customize the look for all of the tenant's users.

▪In this video you you see the creation of an AI model that will be used for item cataloging.

▪By training and using an AI model, it will predict what items are in an uploaded photo of an item.

▪You will also notice how an AI model is used to predict what items are in the item picture and automates the item tagging process.
